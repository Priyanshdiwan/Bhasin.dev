Freelancer Agreement

This Freelance Agreement is made and entered into on this 12/08/24, by and between:

Freelancer: Priyansh Diwan.

Client: Bhasin Automobiles.

Project Description

Development of an eCommerce website forBhasin Automobiles, comprising appox. 12,000 articles, utilizing Amazon S3 servers, SSL, security certification, with the domain purchased by the Client.

1. Scope of Work

The Freelancer agrees to perform the following services:

1. Design and development of an eCommerce website. Integration of 12,000 products (spare parts) into the website.

2. Configuration of Amazon S3 servers for storage and management of product data.

3. Implementation of SSL and other security certifications.

4.Coordination with the Client to integrate the purchased domain.

2. Deliverables

1. Fully functional eCommerce website with all agreed features.

2. Documentation related to the setup of servers, security, and domain integration.

3.Post-deployment support for 52 weeks (included).

3. Payment Terms

Total Project Fee: Rs 43,000/-

Payment Schedule:

1. Rs 20,000 upon signing of the Agreement.

2. Rs 23,000 upon completion of development and deployment.

3. Annual maintainance and renewals of server, certification, storage etc will be charged @ Rs 39,000/- after termination of the post-deployment support .

4. Additional costs for any requested changes outside the initial scope will be billed seperatly.

4. Ownership Rights

Upon full payment, the Client will own the website, including all design, code, and content. The Freelancer retains the right to use the project in their portfolio.

5. Confidentiality

The Freelancer agrees to keep all information related to the project confidential, including proprietary information of Bhasin Motor.
